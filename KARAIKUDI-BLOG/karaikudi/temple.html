TEMPLE

Karpaka Vinayaka Temple or Pillaiyarpatti Pillaiyar Temple is an ancient rock-cut cave shrine dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located at Pillayarpatti in Tiruppathur Taluk, Sivaganga district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. Pilliyarpatti is located 12 kilometers east of Tiruppathur, 3 kilometers west of Kundrakudi Murugan temple and 16 km west of Karaikudi, and 47 km, north-east of Sivaganga town, the district head-quarters.
